Case Study: Dance Community

Dance Courses website design

Project Type

Design Challenge

Individual Project

Role

UX/UI Designer

Tools

Figma (Visual & Interface design)

Duration

Two weeks

BACKGROUND

Project Overview

Dance Community is an original project I created and designed from scratch to create an online platform dedicated to dancers or beginners to join a commmunity and find courses that interests them.
The goal of Dance Community is to provide a user-friendly and engaging online space that caters to the diverse needs of the dance community, from finding classes and booking to courses of interest.

Scope

For this project, I took on the roles of both project manager, UX/UI designer and web developer. Inspired by my own experiences and taking ideas from my dance teacher, who expressed a desire to have a website of her own, I recognized the opportunity to create a centralized hub for the dance community. I conducted research to understand the needs and preferences of dancers, defined the project scope and objectives, and developed a UX/UI strategy to guide the design process.

Project Process

Empathy

Understanding dancer's needs and experiences through discussions and observations.

Ideate

Generating creative design ideas and concepts.

Prototype

Creating tangible design mockups for testing and refinement.

PROJECT STRUCTURE

User Flow

The user flow chart for the Dance Community website outlines the journey that users take as they navigate through the website. It illustrates the key steps and decision points involved in tasks such as finding classes, booking drop in classes, logging in/registering, and information about dance courses and teachers. By visually mapping out the user flow, it ensures a seamless and intuitive interaction with the website.

Sitemap

The sitemap for the Dance Community website provides a hierarchical overview of the site's structure and organization. It outlines the main sections and pages of the website, including Home, About Us, Classes, Drop In Schedule and information about dance courses and teachers. By visually representing the relationships between different pages and sections, the sitemap helps users understand the website's navigation and find relevant content with ease.

DESIGN SOLUTIONS

Style guide

Low-fidelity wireframes

The wireframes for the Dance Community website represent the skeletal framework of the platform, illustrating the layout, structure, and functionality of key pages. I made these low-fidelity representations that focus on the arrangement of content, navigation elements, and user interactions, without incorporating detailed design elements or visuals.

High-fidelity screens

These high-fidelity representations build upon the wireframes I created, incorporating detailed design elements, imagery, typography, and branding to create a polished user interface. I also created the high-fidelity screens for both desktop and mobile modes, to ensure consistency and usability across different devices.
